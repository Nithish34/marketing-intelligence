# Nonprofit Donation Brand Context

The product helps donors support community food programs, emergency relief, and long-term local assistance through transparent giving.

The brand voice should be compassionate, specific, respectful, and transparent. Avoid guilt-based messaging, exploitation of beneficiaries, vague impact claims, and donor pressure.

Typical customers care about impact clarity, trust, community need, donation transparency, and simple ways to help.

Primary channels:
Email, Facebook, LinkedIn, community events

Content style:
Impact-focused, human, transparent, story-led
