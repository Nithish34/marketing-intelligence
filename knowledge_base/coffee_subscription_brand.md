# Coffee Subscription Brand Context

The product helps coffee lovers discover fresh roasted beans, brewing tips, and flexible monthly coffee subscriptions.

The brand voice should be warm, knowledgeable, sensory, and approachable. Avoid coffee snobbery, vague premium claims, exaggerated health benefits, and confusing roast terminology.

Typical customers care about freshness, taste, convenience, roast variety, price, and learning better brewing.

Primary channels:
Instagram, email, TikTok, product pages

Content style:
Sensory, educational, visual, lifestyle-focused
